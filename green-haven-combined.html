About Jordan

The Heart Behind Green Haven Events

Green Haven Events was born from a lifelong love of thoughtful details and meaningful gatherings. I grew up on Green Haven Way in a suburb of Cincinnati, Ohio, in a home where celebration wasn't reserved for big milestones — it was woven into everyday life. My mom believed even the smallest family dinner deserved beautiful table linens, themed touches, and centerpieces that made the moment feel special. Watching her transform ordinary occasions into lasting memories is where my love of details began.

That passion followed me into high school, where I planned homecomings and proms, carefully balancing creativity and budget to bring a vision to life. After college, my career naturally centered around planning — from coordinating educational events for hairdressers to organizing corporate offsite retreats and team-building experiences in the mortgage industry.

For the past 15 years at Marriott, I've had the privilege of planning and executing thousands of events — corporate meetings, galas, weddings, retirement celebrations, showers, and everything in between. I've collaborated closely with clients on menu creation, themes, enhancements, and the small but powerful details that turn an event into an experience.

But for me, this work has never been just about logistics or revenue. It's about connection. It's about helping people express who they are through beautifully curated moments. It's about allowing hosts to be fully present with the people they love, without worrying about execution.

Green Haven Events is the realization of a lifelong dream — to create extraordinary, meaningful gatherings that reflect your personality, your story, and your vision. From a little girl growing up on Green Haven Way to planning your most important moments — this has always been my passion. And I would be honored to bring your vision to life.
